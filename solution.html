Our Solution

The challenge for home-based devices is to minimize unnecessary clinical consultation while not missing the opportunity to intervene if the unborn baby is at risk. There is no reliable way to assess a baby's heart rate at home except with a home-used consumer ultrasound Doppler fetal heart rate device that is notorious in difficulty to find the fetal heart beats, often causing even more alarm to the other. The belt provides continuous heart rate monitoring during the development of fetus from 2nd trimester to 3rd trimester of pregnancy, enabling providers to conduct virtual well-being checks with expectant moms from anywhere.
