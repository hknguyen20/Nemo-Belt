NEMO BELT

A home-based pregnancy monitoring system

Status Quo

There has been a growing demand and implementation of telehealth technology to help pregnant women connect with obstetricians for regular meetings from home.

The Problem

Current telehealth technology have not yet incorporated any techniques to monitor fetal status continuously at home to provide on-site obstetricians with valuable information.

  • Learn more

Solution

We develop Nemo Belt – a wearable device using novel non-contact technology to continuously monitor unborn baby’s wellbeing at home and connect with doctors for remote health monitoring and checkup.

Continuous heart-rate monitoring

Provide live information of fetal well-being via phone-based wireless transmission technology

Virtual wellbeing checks

Leverage the IoT and Big Data to conduct virtual wellbeing checks, delivering detailed insights

User-friendly

Wearable, simple-to-use device with non-intrusive sensors, used anywhere anytime

Affordable

In the same range with other heartbeat counter devices in the market (around $100)

Market Potential

There are no products using the same technology in counting the infants’ heartbeat in Vietnam.
